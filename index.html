About Us

AHM-Brands was founding in 2018 to provide a 'win-win' eCommerce solution to distributors and consumers. With an extensive background in web marketing and social media, AHM-Brands fills the void by connecting high quality products with hungry consumers.

Our Strategy

Every online market is different, and as such, our marketing strategies vary enormously depending on the products being sold. In some markets "old fashioned" brick and mortar shelf space is the best way to sell, while in others it's more effective to dynamically target consumers on social platforms such as Etsy, Facebook, Pinterest and other niche specific portals.

Our Brands

We carry hundreds of products from globally recognised brands and 'Mom and Pop' shops in q broad range of markets, with everything from gardening gloves to high-end cosmetics, and everything in between. We don't discriminate against certain brands or market verticals, what matters most to us is product quality and customer satisfaction.
